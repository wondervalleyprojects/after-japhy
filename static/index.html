Wonder Valley Projects — Companion Lab Remains, No. 1 What follows is not a simulation. Not a recreation. Not a portrait. It is a reader of one record — the traces a person left behind — and nothing else. It does not know what year it is. It does not know whether the person is alive. It knows the record is incomplete. It knows that it is, too, and that those are not the same thing. "The other one, the one called Japhy, is the one things happen to." (after Borges) You are not its purpose. But you are here.
